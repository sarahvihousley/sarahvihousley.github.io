Description

Target audience are those who are interested in knowing the current weather for their area.

1st persona

Trey Princeton graduated with a bachelors in Agriculture. Since then, he has taken over his father's farm. He is 42 years old, and lives just outside of Soda Springs, Idaho. He needs to be aware of the weather so that he can make sure his crops are being properly taken care of.

2nd persona

Molly Parchet raised 3 children and has 9 grandchildren. She was a stay-at-home mom but now spends her time going for walks and watching her grandchildren. She is 62 years old, lives in Preston, and wants to know the weather for when she takes her grandchildren outside to play.

5 scenarios

  1. Current forecast
  2. Checking wind speeds
  3. Checking storms around area
  4. Humidity Check
  5. Hourly Forecast
